ACCURACY OF CHEMICALS DURING WEIGHING & AVOID SPILLAGE OF RAW METERIALS

The objective of this project is to ensure the accurate weighing of chemicals and avoid spillage of raw materials during handling. This improves the precision of formulations, reduces material wastage, and enhances safety by minimizing the risk of chemical spills. It also ensures regulatory compliance and optimizes process efficiency.

Parallel flow mechanism in Heat exchanger in Heat Transfer.

For the parallel flow mechanism in heat exchangers, the primary goal is to understand how heat is transferred when both the hot and cold fluids flow in the same direction. This mechanism impacts heat transfer efficiency and pressure drops, and the objective is to optimize the heat exchanger design for applications in HVAC, power plants, and chemical processes

Determination of overall htc in shell & tube heat exchanger by Chem Cad

The determination of overall heat transfer coefficient (HTC) in a shell & tube heat exchanger by ChemCad aims to accurately calculate the HTC to evaluate the performance of the heat exchanger. By using simulation software like ChemCad, the objective is to determine the thermal efficiency, improve design parameters, and ensure optimal heat transfer rates for industrial processes
